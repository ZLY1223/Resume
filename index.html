Research Interests

My research focuses on Health Informatics, specifically in leveraging system modeling and machine learning to optimize public health interventions, improve resource allocation, and enhance disease surveillance.

Projects

LLM-based Simulation of Vaccine Attitudes

Developed a simulation framework utilizing large language models (LLMs) to predict vaccine hesitancy patterns based on demographic and psychological factors. This model aims to inform targeted public health messaging strategies.

Multi-Pathogen Hospitalization Forecasting

Built a forecasting model integrating time-series epidemiological data to predict hospitalization rates across multiple infectious diseases. Used machine learning techniques like LSTM and ARIMA for accurate predictions.

Disruptive Technology Identification via Multi-Source Data Fusion

Designed a framework combining **scientific publications, patents, and research grants** using **SBERT and Dynamic Topic Modeling (DTM)** to identify emerging technologies in hydrogen energy storage.
